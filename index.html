Our Mission 

Our mission is to represent India on the international stage at the NASA Human Exploration Rover Challenge (HERC) by building a pioneering rover that showcases our technical skills, innovative thinking, and commitment to advancing space exploration. We aim to push the boundaries of engineering and contribute meaningful solutions to challenges in human space exploration.

Our Journey 

We envision a world where technological advancements in space exploration drive educational growth, inspire future innovators, and empower India to play a leading role in the global space industry. Through our participation, we aspire to foster a culture of innovation and collaboration in STEM education across India.
